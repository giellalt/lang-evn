# Nouns
Nouns in Evenki have four sets of suffixes. 
Here we divide them in two groups: V- and C-final ones.

Three stem types

* N_BIRA vowel stems
* N_UMUK consonant stems (voiced and unvoiced)
* N_ORON stems ending in n
